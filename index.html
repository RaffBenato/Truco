Play

Description

The game has 4 players, forming duos, sitting in alternative positions.
Each player gets 3 cards from a traditional deck without, Jokers, 8's, 9's or 10's. The object is to score 12 points before the other duo.

Definitions

Trumps: The highest cards in the game, defined by the Flip Card.
Flip Card: A card that is drawn from the pile and placed face up.
Round: A sequence of 4 plays, where each player plays a card.
Hand: Composed by 2 or 3 rounds, initially are worth 1 point.
Truco: Kind of a bet, where the hand value of points is raised to 3.
Six: A bet that raises the Truco to 6 points.
Nine: A bet that raises the Six to 9 points.
Twelve: A bet that raises the Nine to 12 points.
11's Hand: When one of the teams reach 11 points.
Iron Hand: When both teams reach 11 points
Covered Card: Card with face down and it is worh nothing.
Cards Order (from lowest to highest): 4, 5, 6, 7, Q, J, K, A, 2, 3, Trump Cards.
Suit Order (from lowest to highest): Diamonds, Spades, Hearts and Clubs.

The Game

After cards are distributed, the Flip Card is revealed from the top of the pile. It defines the Trump cards.

Each hand has 3 rounds, where each player plays one card. Whatever team wins the most rounds gets the point. Initially each hand is worth 1 point, at every player's turn they may call Truco to increase the hand to 3 points. The other team then decide if they accept, raise it to 6 or run. If they run the team that called Truco wins. Hands can be raised to Truco, 6, 9 and 12.
If the first round is a draw, whoever wins the second round wins the Hand. If the second round is a draw, whoever won the first round wins the Hand. If the third round is a draw, whoever won the first round wins the Hand. When all 3 rounds are draws, the Hand is cancelled.
At the 11's Hand the players cant ask for Truco, if they do so, they automatically loose the game. The duo with 11 points can see each other's cards and decide if they want to play the hand, if they do, the hand is worth 3 points, if they run the opposing duo gets 1 point.
If both teams reach 11 points then they play the Iron Hand, where all cards stay face down and players play blind.
